Upcoming events in Greece!!

1. Mythical Marathon Festival: Celebrate the spirit of Pheidippides with a marathon event that takes runners through ancient Greek landmarks. Participants will don traditional Greek attire, and the route will pass by historical sites like the Acropolis and the Temple of Zeus.

2. Santorini Starlit Cinema Nights: Experience the magic of open-air cinema against the breathtaking backdrop of Santorini's sunset and the Aegean Sea. Classic Greek films and international favorites will be screened, creating a cinematic paradise under the stars.

Upcoming events in Japan!!

1. Sakura Spectacle Festival: Celebrate the beauty of cherry blossoms with a festival that spans across Tokyo's most iconic parks. Enjoy traditional tea ceremonies, live music performances, and illuminated evening strolls beneath the blooming cherry trees.

2. Sumo Showdown Show: Experience the excitement of sumo wrestling in a special event featuring top-ranked wrestlers in an intimate venue. Fans can get up close to the action, meet the wrestlers, and enjoy traditional Japanese snacks while witnessing the fierce competitions.
